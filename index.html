    Projects Draft

    Smart Glasses Attachment

    To develop a pair of smart glasses capable of displaying notifications, date, and time, as well as translating live audio into captions. Potential enhancements could include GPS tracking for real-time map directions, gesture recognition for hands-free control, and photo and video processing capabilities.

    Kinetic Energy-Powered USB Charger

    To create a small charger that generates power from kinetic movement such as walking or running, suitable for charging USB devices. Potential enhanements could include bluetooth connectivity to monitor energy generation via a mobile app, water resistance for durability against outdoor activities, and solar panel integration during stationary periods.
